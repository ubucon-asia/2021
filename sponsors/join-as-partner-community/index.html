Partner Communities
  • Web

To promote UbuCon Asia event across Asia much further, We’re looking for partner communities. By becoming our partner community, Your community will collaborate with UbuCon Asia team to cross-promote each other’s community and event. This will be a big help for both partner communities and UbuCon Asia to connect with even more diverse people and encourage them to participate and engage.

To learn more about our partner community program, please refer to our brochure. To become our partner community, please contact Coordination team. contact@ubucon.asia
