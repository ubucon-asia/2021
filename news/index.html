National IT Industry Promotion Agency of Korea (NIPA) is a Bronze Sponsor for Ubucon Asia 2021!

 2021-09-18 02:49 PM +0900
  • Vincent Wong

We are proud to announce that the National IT Industry Promotion Agency of Korea (NIPA) is a Bronze Sponsor for Ubucon Asia 2021! NIPA is a national organisation in Korea that actively supports and promotes open source software development. It strengthens software development and provides industry support in a multitude of ways. NIPA is devoted to reinforcing the competitiveness of the ICT industry and contributes to national economic growth through efficient support and laying the groundwork for industrial technology promotion.

Cloudmate Bergabung Sebagai Sponsor Perak Kami!

 2021-08-10 01:49 AM +0700
  • Youngbin Han

Kami dengan senang hati mengumumkan Cloudmate sebagai bagian dari Sponsor Perak. Cloudmate adalah perusahaan CSP (Cloud Service Provider) bersertifikat di Seoul, Korea Selatan. Selain itu, Cloudmate juga menawarkan konsultasi layanan cloud untuk industri yang mengadaptasi infrastruktur cloud untuk pertama kalinya.
